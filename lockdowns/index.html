    NSW Penalties

    It is an offence to not comply with a public health order and the following penalties can apply:

    a maximum penalty of imprisonment for 6 months and/or a penalty of up to $11,000 plus a further $5,500 fine each day the offence continues.

    Corporations that fail to comply with a direction are liable to:

    a fine of $55,000 plus a further $27,500 fine each day the offence continues.

    On the spot fines can also be issued:

    $4,000 for a breach of clause 6 of the Public Health (COVID-19 Border Control) Order 2020 - failing to provide or falsifying information to an enforcement officer $5000 for a breach of the Public Health (COVID-19 Spitting and Coughing) Order (No 2) 2020 $1,000 in other cases.
